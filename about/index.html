I’m Matt, a digital product designer living in Portland, OR. I love working with passionate folks & companies whose purpose extends beyond what they sell.

I specialize in user experience and user interface design. Or in other words, I design beautifully functional applications for screens. My process involves posing questions to users and stakeholders, gathering data, collaborating, pitching, iterating, prototyping, and building.

I am especially passionate about inclusive design, accessibility, and the intersection of design + development. I am also an evironmentalist and believe good design can help mitigate our climate crisis.
